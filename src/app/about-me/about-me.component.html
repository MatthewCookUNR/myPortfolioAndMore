About Me

Hi, I'm Matt! Technology has always had a presence in life since I was a young kid playing games on his Nintendo 64. That passion grew into a career when I decided to study computer science in college and learn about how a piece of this ever-evolving force in our lives really works.


I started working on self-projects after graduation until I found a part-time position at theCoderSchool in Folsom. Teaching the students is a rewarding experience that motivated me to do more with my knowledge. After half a year or so, my technology experience lead me to an even larger opportunity with OnCore Consulting LLC. My current project with OnCore allows me to be a part of a agile development team and expand my experience to an industry level. Most importantly, I can work towards a lifelong accomplishment of mine: using my knowledge to help others.
